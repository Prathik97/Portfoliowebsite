3.5  CASE STUDY 05 — HRMS
Enterprise Workforce & Payroll Platform
From Spreadsheets to a Scalable Operations System
Organisation-wide digital transformation of workforce operations for 500+ part-time and ground staff across four cities.
 Role: Head of Product   ·   Platform: ERPNext   ·   Hyderabad · Bengaluru · Pune · Vijayawada 

THE PROBLEM
 No System of Record   ·   Payroll Leakage   ·   Asset Not Recovered   ·   Attendance Unreliable   ·   Zero Visibility 

7 CAPABILITIES DELIVERED
Workforce Master Data & Role Architecture — configurable org model, departments, cost centres
Onboarding & Compliance Workflow — digital consent, document storage, biometric provisioning
Attendance & Regularisation Engine — hybrid biometric + mobile, 24hr regularisation window
Payroll & Incentive Rule Engine — multi-variable rules, configurable by business, no redeployment needed
Asset Lifecycle & Recovery — catalogue, assignment, deduction enforcement at exit
Workforce Visibility Dashboards — by city, location and role for leadership
Worker Self-Service App — payslips, attendance, leave requests, asset liability, advance pay

DESIGN HIGHLIGHT — INCENTIVE RULE ENGINE
The most complex capability — configurable by business users without engineering.

Attendance: Leave days ≤ 2/month → eligible for full incentive
Punch-in: Before 4:15 AM → ₹200 component unlocked
Issue-based: 0 tickets = ₹300, 5+ tickets = ₹0
Seasonal override: Diwali attendance-only mode, time-window activation
