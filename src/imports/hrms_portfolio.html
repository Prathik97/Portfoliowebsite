Enterprise
Workforce
& Payroll
Platform

Organisation-wide digital transformation of workforce operations — onboarding, attendance, payroll, incentives, asset tracking and exits — for part-time and ground staff across four cities.

Role: AVP – Product (Head of Product)
Platform: ERPNext (Custom Build)
Cities: Hyderabad · Bengaluru · Pune · Vijayawada
Functions: HR · Supply Chain · Operations · Finance
The Problem

Manual. Fragmented.
Unscalable.

Before this initiative, the organisation managed its entire part-time and ground workforce through spreadsheets and physical files. As the workforce scaled across cities, the cracks turned into crises — in payroll accuracy, compliance, operational visibility and asset recovery.

📋
No System of Record
All employee and partner data maintained in disconnected spreadsheets and physical hard copies across locations.
🕐
Attendance Unreliable
Biometric capture disconnected from payroll; manual tracking prone to error and manipulation.
💸
Payroll Leakage
Incentive and bonus calculations done manually with no audit trail — error-prone and opaque to employees.
📦
Asset Not Recovered
Issued assets (bags, equipment) not tracked in any system — exiting workers rarely returned them.
🗺️
Zero Leadership Visibility
No real-time view of workforce availability by city, location or role for planning and hiring.
⚖️
Compliance Risk
Onboarding and exit processes uncontrolled, undocumented and non-auditable across all locations.

Design Highlight

Payroll & Incentive
Rule Engine

The most complex and differentiating capability — a configurable rule engine allowing business users to define, modify and activate incentive structures without engineering involvement.

Incentive Rules
Multi-Variable Incentive Programmes
Attendance: Leave days ≤ 2/month → eligible for full incentive component
Punch-in time: Before 4:15 AM → ₹200 component unlocked
Issue-based: Tiered deductions — 0 tickets = ₹300, 5+ tickets = ₹0
Seasonal override: Time-window activation — e.g. Diwali attendance-only mode
Bonus Rules
Configurable Bonus Engines
Packet bonus: >3,000 qualifying packets/month → ₹1 per packet above threshold
Long route: Total km > 30 → ₹3 per extra km; both threshold and rate configurable
Tray bonus: Monthly trays ÷ 24 × ₹10 for Cluster DP role
Referral: ₹500 payout to referring partner after 2 months active tenure
Deductions & Advance
Automated Settlement Logic
Advance pay: Pro-rated by days worked; approval via zonal head within 7 days
Asset deductions: Bag not returned → configured value auto-deducted at exit
Absence deductions: Unapproved absent days deducted from monthly salary
Full & final: All pending amounts reconciled and settled at exit
Operating Model

Governance &
Risk Design

I designed the ownership model across all functions and built failure handling directly into the product — not as an afterthought.

Area Ownership
Onboarding & Docs Ground teams (maker) → HR verification (checker); all features gated on HR approval
Attendance & Regularisation Partner self-service → Zonal head (24-hr window) → HR
Payroll & Settlements Central HR ownership; processed only after attendance confirmed
Asset Issuance & Recovery Operations at onboarding + HR deductions at exit
System Exceptions HR and Ops jointly; daily sync monitoring + manual fallback
Daily Attendance Sync Monitoring
Automated alerting for drift; manual fallback workflows when biometric devices or connectivity fail.
Mobile App as Biometric Backup
Designed so device dependency doesn't break the system — mobile punch-in operates independently.
Post-Go-Live Control Metrics
80% punch-in/out compliance target at 2 months. Failure triggers mandatory regularisation and salary deduction enforcement — designed in before launch.
Phase-Gated Rollout
Payslips and regularisation deferred to Phase 2 to validate adoption before adding compliance burden to ground teams.
